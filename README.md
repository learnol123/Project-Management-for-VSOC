Project Scenario : 
The CEO of a cybersecurity technology startup, is hiring you to work on a strategic project supporting a fleet of connected and autonomous vehicles. The company is
building out a Vehicle Security Operations Center (VSOC). The vision for the product is to build a cloud-based plaIorm to support the following capabilities:
1. A real-time monitoring platform that ingests, analyzes, and responds to streaming data
from fleets of connected and autonomous vehicles.
2. Reporting of cyber threats in real time
3. Automated real-time responses to cyber threats
4. Technical and executive reports identifying security gaps and recommended fixes
5. Secure data pipelines for regulatory compliance and auditing
Project Stakeholders
1. Security and operational analysts who will be the primary users of the system
2. Data engineers and DevOps engineers are responsible for the technical system
implementation.
3. The legal and compliance team is responsible for data privacy and legal/regulatory
compliance.
